Kilo Lima Message Composer

Privacy Policy

Kilo Lima Message Composer is an internal authoring tool used by our development team. It is built to know as little about you as possible. This Privacy Policy describes what little data the app touches and how it handles it.

Effective: July 7, 2026

Information We Do Not Collect

Kilo Lima Management LLC does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifying information about you. The app does not require an account, has no login, does not embed analytics or advertising frameworks, and does not transmit usage telemetry. There is no server-side user record to view, export, or delete because none exists.

Local Data Storage

The only credential the app stores is an optional GitHub Personal Access Token that the operator enters manually in Settings. It is your own credential for your own repository, and it is stored on your device in the Apple Keychain. It is never transmitted anywhere except directly to GitHub (api.github.com) when you choose to publish, and it is used for no other purpose. You may remove it at any time in Settings, or by deleting the app.

Messages you compose are held on your device while you edit them and are written only to your own repository when you publish. The app keeps no other saved user data.

Network Connections

The app makes only these outbound connections, all over standard HTTPS:

• api.github.com — to read and write the message file in your own private repository, and only when a token is present.
• itunes.apple.com/lookup — to read the current public App Store version of a target app, a safeguard used only during composition.
• kilolimallc.github.io — to fetch our own currently-published, public message file for the “Live on GitHub” view.

None of these connections sends personal information about you.

Location

Kilo Lima Message Composer does not request, use, or transmit your device's location.

Tracking

The app does not track you and does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework. No data is used to follow you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Third-Party Services

When you publish, the app communicates with GitHub using your own access token; GitHub's handling of that request is governed by GitHub's privacy practices. Apple processes the App Store version lookup described above. Kilo Lima Management LLC receives none of your personal data from either service.

Internal Distribution

This app is an internal developer tool distributed privately to our organization through Apple Business Manager. It is not offered to, or intended for, the general public.

Children's Privacy

The app is an internal tool for our development team and is not directed at children under 13. Kilo Lima Management LLC does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Kilo Lima Management LLC may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will be available at this URL. Material changes will be reflected in the Effective Date above.
