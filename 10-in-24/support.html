10 IN 24 Support An FAA Part 135 § 135.267 flight-time and rest-period tracker. Built for solo charter pilots and small Part 135 operators who need a clear running picture of where they stand against the 10-hours-in-24 rest requirement. Contact Questions, bug reports, feature requests, or feedback — any of it is welcome. The fastest way to reach support is by email. kilolimallc@icloud.com You can also reach the same address from inside the app via the in-app feedback option, which pre-fills your app version, iOS version, and device model so triage is faster. Before You Write If you're reporting an unexpected compliance reading or calculation, it helps a lot if you can include: • The specific date and duty period the issue applies to. • The exact start and end times you entered (including timezone — local or Zulu). • Flight time(s) and rest period(s) logged for the relevant 24-hour window. • The compliance reading you saw — and what you expected the app to show. Screenshots of the affected screens are usually faster than describing the layout in text. Documentation The in-app Help and Disclaimer sheets cover the app's intended use as an advisory aid to the operator's required records — not as a substitute for them. Always retain the records required by 14 CFR Part 135 in the form your operator requires.
