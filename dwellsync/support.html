Common questions

How do I connect my lights? In the app, open Devices → Connect a light brand and follow the steps to get your key. Govee is supported today; more brands are on the way.

Why didn't my lights change on schedule? Cloud control depends on your manufacturer's service and an internet connection, and is subject to their rate limits. See the Disclaimer.
