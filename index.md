# Privacy Policy — 10 in 24

**Effective date:** 2026-06-01

This Privacy Policy describes how Kilo Lima Management LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the **10 in 24** iOS app ("the app"). We've designed the app to need as little data as possible. In short: **the app stores all of your planning data locally on your device and does not send it to us or to any third party.**

## 1. Who we are

**Kilo Lima Management LLC**
Contact: **kilolimallc@icloud.com**

## 2. Information the app stores on your device

The app stores the following information **on your device only**, using Apple's standard on-device storage (SwiftData). This information is not transmitted to us or to any server we control.

- **Settings you configure** — crew size, default duty offsets, alert thresholds, Quick Turn minimums, rest-period tier values.
- **Duty period and flight data you enter** — block-out and block-in times, completion status, per-duty show/release overrides, custom duty start/end.
- **Onboarding flags** — whether you have acknowledged the in-app disclaimer, completed first-launch setup, and seen the v2 transition explainer.

Deleting the app from your device deletes all of this information.

## 3. Information that goes to Apple (and only to Apple)

The app uses Apple's standard system services. Information exchanged with these services is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/), not this one.

- **App Store / StoreKit (subscriptions and legacy access detection)** — When you purchase, restore, or manage a subscription, Apple handles the transaction. We do not see, store, or process your payment method, billing address, or Apple ID password. We do receive from Apple's StoreKit framework an anonymous indication of whether you have an active subscription (and which tier) or are a legacy purchaser of the original one-time version of the app.
- **App Transaction receipt** — The app reads the build number of your original purchase from Apple's `AppTransaction` system service to determine whether you qualify for grandfathered "legacy" access. This check happens entirely on your device.

## 4. Information you may choose to send us

The app does **not** automatically transmit any information to us. The only way information reaches us is if you explicitly choose to contact us:

- **Send Feedback / Get Help (from About → Send Feedback)** — Opens your device's mail app with a pre-filled email to `kilolimallc@icloud.com` that includes the app version, your iOS version, and your device model identifier (e.g. "iPhone15,3"). **You must tap Send** for the email to leave your device. The body of the email contains only what you type plus the diagnostic information above. We use email you send us solely to respond to your request.

## 5. Information we do NOT collect

To be explicit: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Your name, account credentials, or personal identifiers
- Your location
- Your contacts, calendar, photos, or files
- Your flight records, duty data, or any planning input you enter into the app
- Analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or usage tracking
- Advertising identifiers
- Cookies or web tracking technologies (the app does not embed a web view for tracking)

The app contains no third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, attribution, or social-network integration.

## 6. Data retention

Because we do not receive your data, we have no retention period for it. The planning data you enter remains on your device until you delete it (per-record or by deleting the app). Subscription records are retained by Apple in accordance with Apple's policies.

If you email us (Section 4 above), we retain that email and our reply for as long as needed to handle your request, and then delete it. We do not use email addresses you send us for marketing or any other purpose.

## 7. Children

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, so by definition we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has interacted with us, please contact us at the address above.

## 8. Your rights

Because the app stores your information only on your device, you have direct, complete control over it:

- **To view or modify your data:** open the app.
- **To delete your data:** delete records in-app, or delete the app from your device to remove everything.
- **To stop sharing diagnostic data with us:** don't send feedback emails.

If you reside in a jurisdiction that grants additional rights regarding personal data (such as the European Union's GDPR or California's CCPA/CPRA): because we do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data, most such rights do not apply to interactions with the app. For any inquiry related to data you have voluntarily emailed us, contact us at the address above.

## 9. Security

The data stored on your device is protected by your device's standard iOS security model, including device-level encryption when you have a passcode set. We recommend keeping a passcode, biometric authentication, and current iOS version on your device.

## 10. International users

The app is published from the United States. If you use the app outside the United States, your information remains on your device — there is no cross-border transfer. If you email us, your message reaches our email provider (Apple iCloud Mail) under their applicable terms.

## 11. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Effective date" at the top and may also notify you via the app, the App Store listing, or the email address you have used to contact us (if any). Continued use of the app after a change indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
