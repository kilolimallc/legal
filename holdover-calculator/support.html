Before You Write

If you're reporting an unexpected result, it helps a lot if you can include:

• The METAR you were working with (or the manual conditions you entered).
• The fluid type, concentration, and brand.
• The result you saw — and what you expected to see.
• Which regulator (FAA or Transport Canada) you have selected in Settings.
