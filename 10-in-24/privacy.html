10 IN 24 Privacy Policy 10 in 24 is built around a single principle: the app should know as little about you as possible. This Privacy Policy describes what little data the app touches and how it handles it. Effective: May 22, 2026 Information We Do Not Collect The publisher does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifying information about you. The app does not require an account, does not embed analytics or advertising frameworks, and does not transmit usage telemetry. There is no server-side user record to view, export, or delete because none exists. Location 10 in 24 does not request, use, or transmit your device's location. Duty and flight-time computations are derived entirely from the times and durations you enter manually. Local Data Storage Duty periods, flight legs, rest periods, and any saved aircraft or operator profiles are stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you have iCloud device backups enabled in iOS Settings, in which case it is included in your backup under Apple's standard iCloud privacy practices. You may delete all locally stored data at any time by deleting the app. Third-Party Services 10 in 24 does not communicate with any third-party API or remote service. All rest-compliance computations are performed locally on your device using the regulatory references embedded in the app. Subscriptions Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The app receives only a verified entitlement status from Apple's StoreKit framework — payment details, your Apple ID, and personal billing information remain with Apple at all times. Apple's privacy practices for these transactions are described at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy. Children's Privacy The app is intended for use by adult aviation professionals and is not directed at children under 13. The publisher does not knowingly collect information from children.
