HOLDOVER CALCULATOR Support An aviation anti-icing holdover and allowance time advisor based on FAA and Transport Canada published guidance. Built for the pre-departure decision. Contact Questions, bug reports, feature requests, or feedback — any of it is welcome. The fastest way to reach support is by email. kilolimallc@icloud.com You can also reach the same address from inside the app: tap the … menu in the top-right and choose Send Feedback / Get Help. The in-app version pre-fills your app version, iOS version, and device model so triage is faster. Before You Write If you're reporting an unexpected result, it helps a lot if you can include: • The METAR you were working with (or the manual conditions you entered). • The fluid type, concentration, and brand. • The result you saw — and what you expected to see. • Which regulator (FAA or Transport Canada) you have selected in Settings. Documentation The in-app Help sheet (toolbar menu → Help) walks through the interview, the result view, and the timer. The Disclaimer sheet covers the legal scope of the app as an advisory aid only.
