Their availability, features, and behavior are outside our control and may change or be interrupted. You are responsible for the safe installation, wiring, weatherproofing, and use of your lighting hardware — especially outdoor and do-it-yourself installations (for example, WLED-based addressable strips). Follow the manufacturer's instructions and all applicable electrical codes. Dwellsync does not verify that your hardware is installed, rated, or suitable for its location.

Schedules and Automation

Automatic on/off and holiday scheduling are provided on a best-effort basis and depend on your device, your network, the manufacturer's service, and (for cloud scheduling) our backend. Do not depend on a scene turning on or off at an exact time. Verify any behavior that matters to you.

Color plans you publish are visible to other users, and plans you import were authored by others. Review any imported plan before applying it to your lights.
