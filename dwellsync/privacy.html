Dwellsync

Privacy Policy

Dwellsync helps you design, schedule, and control your smart lights, and optionally share lighting plans with others. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

Effective: July 9, 2026

Summary

You can use Dwellsync's editor and scheduler, and control your own lights, without creating an account. If you choose to sign in, we store your account email, the lighting plans you save, basic information about your lighting devices, and — only if you opt in — an encrypted copy of the manufacturer API key needed to run your schedules. We do not sell your data, we do not use third-party advertising, and we do not embed third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect the email address and password you provide. Authentication is handled by our backend provider, Supabase; passwords are stored by Supabase in salted, hashed form and are never visible to us. Your email is used to sign you in, to associate your saved plans with your account, and, where you publish a plan, to attribute it by your chosen username.

Lighting Plans and Shared Content

Lighting plans you save (colors, sequences, and schedules) are stored in our database so they sync to your account. If you mark a plan public, that plan and your username become visible to other Dwellsync users, who may view or copy ("fork") it. Plans you keep private are visible only to you. You can delete any plan at any time.

Device Information

When you connect lights, Dwellsync stores non-sensitive metadata about your devices — such as the device name you gave them, the model, the manufacturer's device identifier, and the device's capabilities (for example, how many color segments it has). This lets your devices and automations sync across your sessions. Dwellsync does not collect your home address or the physical location of your lights.

Manufacturer API Keys

To control your lights, Dwellsync uses a credential you obtain from your light's manufacturer (for example, a Govee API key). On your device, this key is stored locally. If you enable cloud scheduling — which lets your schedules run when the app is closed — the key is uploaded to our backend and stored encrypted at rest, decrypted only by the scheduled server process that sends commands to your lights on your behalf. Your key is never shown to other users and is never included in a shared plan. You can remove it at any time.

Third-Party Services

Dwellsync relies on the following third parties, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — hosts our authentication, database, and scheduled functions. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Light manufacturers you connect (for example, Govee) — when you control your lights, the commands you send and the API key you provide are transmitted to that manufacturer's service so it can operate your devices. Your use of their service is governed by their terms and privacy policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Dwellsync does not embed third-party advertising or analytics/tracking frameworks, does not build advertising profiles, and does not request or transmit your device's geographic location. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your account data for as long as your account exists. You can delete individual plans and devices from within the app. To delete your account and associated data, email us at the address below and we will remove your record. Deleting the app from your device removes locally stored data (including any locally saved API key).

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and manufacturer API keys held for cloud scheduling are encrypted at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Dwellsync is not directed to children under 13, and Kilo Lima Management LLC does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
