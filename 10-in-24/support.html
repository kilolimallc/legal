Before You Write

If you're reporting an unexpected compliance reading or calculation, it helps a lot if you can include:

• The specific date and duty period the issue applies to.
• The exact start and end times you entered (including timezone — local or Zulu).
• Flight time(s) and rest period(s) logged for the relevant 24-hour window.
• The compliance reading you saw — and what you expected the app to show.

Screenshots of the affected screens are usually faster than describing the layout in text.

Documentation

The in-app Help and Disclaimer sheets cover the app's intended use as an advisory aid to the operator's required records — not as a substitute for them. Always retain the records required by 14 CFR Part 135 in the form your operator requires.
